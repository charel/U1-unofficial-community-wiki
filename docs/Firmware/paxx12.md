# Custom Firmware
You can install a custom firmware on your U1 to enable more features. 
Find the documentation [here](https://snapmakeru1-extended-firmware.pages.dev).

## Warning!
While installing custom firmware does not automatically void the product warranty, any damage caused by or attributable to the installation or use of custom firmware is not covered under warranty. Use at your own risk. See [Snapmaker Terms of Use](https://www.snapmaker.com/terms-of-use) for details.

Custom firmware is intended for users with appropriate technical knowledge. Ensure you understand the implications before proceeding.
